Who We Are

From our beginnings, Maxwell Scientific has grown to become a leading supplier of science teaching materials. Today, from our headquarters in Marion, Illinois, we serve customers worldwide, including teachers, professors, home-school educators, and professionals in health and science-related fields.

Our Vision

To provide educators the finest products and services that have been tested in-house, to help students of all ages learn and understand science.

Today

Maxwell Scientific is truly one of the most hands-on companies in the world. We work in partnership with educators and scientists to provide everything needed for science education, including:

  • Crystal Radio Kit - Teach the principles of radios and electronics with this special radio kit. No batteries required.

  • Bond Adhesive - Maxwell Scientific's special adhesive is perfect for toothbridge bridge and towers. It dries quickly, providing a rigid bond. Nontoxic.

  • Water Test Kit - Perform four interesting water pollution experiments with this water testing kit.
